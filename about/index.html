About Noble Acer

Noble Acre is founded by three industry veterans with a shared passion for revolutionizing the real estate experience. Each founder brings over two decades of expertise in finance, real estate, customer management and marketing, recognizing that successful property transactions and financing demand more than mere listings—they require strategic insights and a client-first approach.

Our journey began with a clear vision: to create a platform that not only ensures seamless property transactions but also serves as a trusted advisor for all real estate needs, including personalized loan facility and property management. Today, we proudly stand as an industry thought leader, offering comprehensive transactional support and in-depth advisory services that encompass every aspect of property ownership.



Our Team

Our leadership team at Noble Acre is comprised of three founders whose diverse backgrounds form the foundation of our company. Their combined experience ensures that every client receives unparalleled guidance, encompassing market analysis, investment strategies, property financing, and closing deals.



Our Services Include

  1. Residential and Commercial Property Transactions: Expert handling of property acquisitions, sales, and leases.

  2. Market Consultation & Investment Advisory: In-depth market analysis and strategic investment guidance.

  3. Customized Property Solutions & Financial Planning: Tailored real estate solutions and comprehensive financial planning.

  4. Tailored Loan Facilities & Financing Support: Personalized loan arrangements and financing assistance.

  5. Real Estate Strategy & Negotiation Support: Strategic planning and negotiation services for optimal outcomes.
